Falafel Al Quds

Al-Quds Falafel is a popular spot on Rainbow Street in Jabal Amman, Jordan, serving authentic Middle Eastern fast food. With a 4.5-star rating from 385 reviews, it’s known for delicious falafel, vegetarian-friendly and vegan options, and Halal dishes. Open daily for breakfast, lunch, dinner, and late-night meals, it’s a favorite for locals and visitors alike.
