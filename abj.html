Abu Jbara

Abu Jbara Restaurant in Amman is known for its fresh, traditional Jordanian dishes, including falafel, hummus, and foul. Open daily from morning to midnight, it offers vegetarian and halal options, a cozy atmosphere, and friendly staff. While popular and well-loved for its authentic flavors, the restaurant can get crowded and service may sometimes be slow.

More Details

Has Many Vegetarian friendly, Vegan friendly, and Halal options
