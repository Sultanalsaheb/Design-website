Jarash Roman Ruins

Jerash is one of the best-preserved Roman cities outside Italy, showcasing majestic arches, colonnaded streets, sprawling plazas, and ancient theaters. Walking through the ruins, visitors can imagine life in a vibrant Roman city. Highlights include Hadrian’s Gate, the Oval Plaza, the Temple of Artemis, and the South Theatre, which boasts remarkable acoustics. Jerash is ideal for history lovers, offering a glimpse into Jordan’s Greco-Roman past and unforgettable views from elevated spots across the site.
