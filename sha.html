Khashoka

Khashoka is a Middle Eastern restaurant in Amman, rated 4.3/5. It offers vegetarian, vegan, and halal options, serves breakfast to late night, and is known for tasty food and a welcoming atmosphere. Some reviews mention slow service or small portions.
